# Sales & Shipment Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, shipment trends, product profitability, and salesperson performance. The dashboard transforms raw sales and shipment data into actionable business insights, enabling data-driven decision-making through intuitive visualizations and KPI monitoring.

## Objectives

* Monitor key business performance indicators.
* Analyze sales and shipment trends over time.
* Identify top-performing products and sales representatives.
* Evaluate product-level profitability.
* Support business decisions with interactive reporting and analytics.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* SQL
* Microsoft Excel
* Power Query
* DAX

## Key Features

### Executive KPI Dashboard

* Total Revenue
* Total Boxes Shipped
* Total Shipment Count
* Total Profit
* Profit Percentage

### Sales Trend Analysis

* Monthly sales performance comparison.
* Year-over-Year trend monitoring.
* Revenue growth visualization.

### Shipment Analysis

* Shipment volume distribution.
* Product movement tracking.
* Operational performance monitoring.

### Product Analytics

* Top-selling products analysis.
* Product-wise revenue contribution.
* Profitability analysis across products.

### Salesperson Performance

* Ranking of top sales representatives.
* Revenue and shipment contribution analysis.
* Individual profit performance tracking.

### Geographic Analysis

* Country-wise sales distribution.
* Regional contribution comparison.
* Market performance insights.

## Business Insights Generated

* Identified high-performing products driving revenue growth.
* Evaluated profit margins across product categories.
* Tracked shipment efficiency and operational trends.
* Compared salesperson performance using revenue and profit metrics.
* Supported strategic decision-making through KPI-based reporting.

## Project Outcome

The dashboard provides a centralized reporting solution that enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify growth opportunities, and make informed decisions using real-time visual analytics.
